How to Get in Touch

There are several ways to get in touch.

  • Our main communication channel is our Gitter chatroom. Join us there with your GitHub username or Twitter handle to join in the conversation.
  • Join the Library Carpentry Topicbox Group.
  • If you are new to the community, we have an engagement form - fill that in to tell us what you want to do.
  • Want a Library Carpentry workshop at your institution? Request one. (The Carpentries are helping us with this process for now.)
  • You can raise issues on any of the lesson repositories or against this site through the Library Carpentry GitHub organisation.
