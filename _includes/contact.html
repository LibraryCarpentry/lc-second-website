How to Get in Touch

Our main channel is our Gitter chatroom. Join us there with your GitHub username or Twitter handle to join in the conversation.

Join the Library Carpentry Google Group.

We also have an engagement form - fill that in to tell us what you want to do.

Want a Library Carpentry workshop at your institution? Request one. (The Carpentries are helping us with this process for now.)

You can raise issues on any of the lesson repositories or against this site through the Library Carpentry GitHub organisation.
